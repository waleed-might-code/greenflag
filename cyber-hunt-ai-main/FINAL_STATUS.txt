HOW THIS ENABLES "MINING INSANE AMOUNT OF DATA"
================================================================================

PROBLEM SOLVED:
  Before: Data mining without provenance = unusable for enterprise
  After: Every data point traceable to raw HTTP captures

DATA FLOW:
  Crawler discovers endpoint
      ↓
  HTTP request/response captured to WARC (SHA-256 ID)
      ↓
  Test executes, captures more HTTP to WARC
      ↓
  Finding created with evidence_warc_refs = [WARC URIs]
      ↓
  Report generated with evidence packs
      ↓
  RESULT: Massive, queryable, audit-ready data dump
